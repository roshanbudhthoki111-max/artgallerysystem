QR Image Folder
===============

Drop your personal payment QR screenshots here with these exact filenames:

  esewa_personal.png   — Your personal eSewa QR code image
  khalti_personal.png  — Your personal Khalti QR code image

How to get your personal QR:
  eSewa:  Open eSewa app → My QR (or Receive Money) → screenshot it
  Khalti: Open Khalti app → My QR → screenshot it

The payment page will automatically show your personal QR
instead of the generated one as soon as the file exists here.

⚠️  With personal QR, customers must enter the amount manually —
    the QR does not encode a specific amount.
